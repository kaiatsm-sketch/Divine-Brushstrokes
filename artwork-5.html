Unspoken Words

This intimate work explores the eternal dialogue between gesture and restraint—a meditation on natural boundaries and transitions rendered in rich oil.

Gestural Echoes

The upper region reveals layered glazes that evoke shifting human expression—mist, cloud, and light merging in subtle gradations of color and tone.

The Space Between

Central forms suggest the rhythmic push and pull of tides, painted with fluid brushwork that echoes the dialogue's eternal movement and power.

Whispered Marks

The lower portion holds darker, more grounded tones—remnants of what the conversation leaves behind, speaking to impermanence and transformation.

A Silent Exchange

Together, these elements create a dreamlike composition—neither purely representational nor fully abstract, but something felt and remembered.
