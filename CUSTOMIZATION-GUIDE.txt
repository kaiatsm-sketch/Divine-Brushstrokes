# COMPLETE CUSTOMIZATION GUIDE

## üéØ How to Control Zoom & Focus Points

In each `<section>` tag, you control zoom with three attributes:

```html
<section class="scroll-section detail-section" data-zoom="2.0" data-x="30" data-y="20">
```

### `data-zoom` - How Much to Zoom
- `1` = No zoom (full artwork visible)
- `1.5` = Zoom in 1.5x
- `2.0` = Zoom in 2x  
- `2.5` = Zoom in 2.5x (very close)
- Higher numbers = closer zoom

### `data-x` - Horizontal Focus (Left to Right)
- `0` = Far left edge
- `25` = Left quarter
- `50` = Center
- `75` = Right quarter
- `100` = Far right edge

### `data-y` - Vertical Focus (Top to Bottom)
- `0` = Top edge
- `25` = Upper quarter
- `50` = Center (middle)
- `75` = Lower quarter  
- `100` = Bottom edge

### Example Focus Points:
```html
<!-- Zoom to top-left corner -->
<section data-zoom="2.0" data-x="25" data-y="25">

<!-- Zoom to center -->
<section data-zoom="1.8" data-x="50" data-y="50">

<!-- Zoom to bottom-right detail -->
<section data-zoom="2.2" data-x="75" data-y="80">
```

### 2. Pop-up Images (Portraits, References, Details)
Find sections with pop-up images:
```html
<div class="popup-image from-left portrait">
    <img src="YOUR_IMAGE_URL_HERE" alt="Description">
    <div class="caption-bubble top-left">
        Your caption text here!
    </div>
</div>
```

Replace `YOUR_IMAGE_URL_HERE` with your image.

## üí¨ How to Add Pop-Up Images (Comic Style)

### Basic Structure:
```html
<div class="popup-image from-left medium">
    <img src="path/to/image.jpg" alt="Description">
    <div class="caption-bubble top-left">
        Your text here!
    </div>
</div>
```

### Direction Options (where it comes from):
- `from-left` - Slides in from left side
- `from-right` - Slides in from right side
- `from-top` - Drops down from top
- `from-bottom` - Pops up from bottom

### Size Options:
- `small` - 200x200px
- `medium` - 300x300px  
- `large` - 400x400px
- `portrait` - 250x350px (tall)
- `landscape` - 400x250px (wide)

### Caption Bubble Position:
- `top-left` - Speech bubble points to top-left
- `bottom-right` - Speech bubble points to bottom-right

### Full Example - Multiple Pop-ups in One Section:
```html
<section class="scroll-section detail-section" data-zoom="2.0" data-x="60" data-y="40">
    <!-- Your text description -->
    <div class="text-overlay left">
        <h2>Influences</h2>
        <p>This section was influenced by Picasso and Matisse.</p>
    </div>
    
    <!-- Pop-up 1: Picasso portrait from left -->
    <div class="popup-image from-left portrait" style="top: 15%;">
        <img src="images/picasso-portrait.jpg" alt="Picasso">
        <div class="caption-bubble top-left" style="top: -60px; left: 10px;">
            Picasso's cubism!
        </div>
    </div>
    
    <!-- Pop-up 2: Matisse work from right -->
    <div class="popup-image from-right medium" style="top: 50%;">
        <img src="images/matisse-cutout.jpg" alt="Matisse">
        <div class="caption-bubble bottom-right" style="bottom: -60px; right: 10px;">
            Matisse's color
        </div>
    </div>
    
    <!-- Pop-up 3: Detail shot from bottom -->
    <div class="popup-image from-bottom small">
        <img src="images/detail-closeup.jpg" alt="Detail">
    </div>
</section>
```

## üìç Advanced Positioning

### Fine-tune Pop-up Position:
Add inline styles to adjust exactly where pop-ups appear:

```html
<!-- Control vertical position -->
<div class="popup-image from-left medium" style="top: 20%;">

<!-- Or use bottom positioning -->
<div class="popup-image from-right small" style="bottom: 15%; right: 8%;">
```

Percentages:
- `top: 10%` = Near top of screen
- `top: 50%` = Middle of screen
- `bottom: 20%` = Near bottom
- `left: 5%` / `right: 5%` = Horizontal position

## üí° Tips for Best Results

1. **Start with zoom=1** (full view) then gradually zoom in
2. **Use odd numbers of pop-ups** (1, 3, 5) - looks better
3. **Mix sizes** - combine small, medium, large for variety
4. **Don't overcrowd** - 1-3 pop-ups per section is ideal
5. **Test on mobile** - pop-ups auto-resize but check positioning
6. **Vary directions** - alternate from-left, from-right, etc.
