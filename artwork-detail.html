MIXED MEDIA ON CANVAS, 2024

SCROLL TO EXPLORE ↓

A Journey Through Light

This piece explores the interplay between light and shadow, capturing fleeting moments of beauty that exist in the space between clarity and ambiguity.

Textural Complexity

Notice the layered brushwork in the upper portion—each stroke was applied with intention, building depth through translucent glazes and bold impasto techniques.

The Heart of the Composition

At the center, warm amber tones emerge from cooler surroundings. This contrast represents the tension between chaos and calm, a central theme throughout the work.

Gestural Energy

The lower section reveals dynamic, gestural marks that suggest movement and spontaneity—a counterpoint to the more controlled areas above.

The Complete Vision

Stepping back, the composition reveals its full narrative—a meditation on memory, emotion, and the ephemeral nature of human experience.
